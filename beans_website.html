Our Story

Born from a love of the humble bean.

It started in 1998 when founder Elena Reyes returned from a trip to Oaxaca, Mexico, carrying a small bag of dried heirloom beans that a local farmer had gifted her. The flavour was unlike anything she'd tasted — complex, earthy, and alive in a way that supermarket beans simply weren't.

She spent the next three years tracking down small-batch farmers across four continents, learning their stories and forging relationships built on respect, fairness, and a shared passion for extraordinary legumes.

Today, The Bean Barn works with 28 farm families across 12 countries, bringing the world's most extraordinary beans to cooks everywhere.

Est. 1998

What We Stand For

Our Mission & Values

🌱 Sustainability First

We believe that healthy soil grows healthy beans. Every farm partner meets our rigorous sustainability standards — protecting land, water, and communities for generations to come.

🤝 Fair Trade Always

We pay farmers 40% above market rate. When farmers thrive, their families thrive, their communities thrive, and their traditions survive. That's the kind of supply chain we're proud of.

🔬 Quality Obsession

Every batch is tested for moisture content, seed integrity, and flavour profile before it ever reaches your kitchen. We reject anything less than extraordinary.
